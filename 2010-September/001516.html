Hm, regarding

http://www.kvaser.com/en/products/can/pci.html

the PCIcanx II HS/HS is an active CAN card

# On-board 16-bit microcontroller
# Dimensions 120 x 95 mm (approx. 4.5 x 4 in.)
# Communicates with the PC through a fast DPRAM
# Quick and easy plug-and-play installation - no switches.
# CAN Controller: Renesas M16C

For that reason the kvaser_pci driver for the SJA1000 based cards (PCIcanx
HS/HS and PCIcan HS/HS) won't work with your hardware.

Maybe you can make the Kvaser originated Linux driver a SocketCAN compatible
driver ;-)
