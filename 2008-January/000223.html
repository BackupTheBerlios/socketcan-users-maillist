Hi all,

today Linus Torvalds pulled the net-2.6.25 networking development tree
from David S. Miller into the linux-2.6.git (Linux Mainline repository)

http://git.kernel.org/?p=linux/kernel/git/torvalds/linux-2.6.git;a=commit;h=0ba6c33bcddc64a54b5f1c25a696c4767dc76292

So from now on SocketCAN definitely touched the ground: The eagle has
landed ;-)

The latest snapshot from www.kernel.org (2.6.24-git6) therefore also
contains the new CAN subsystem.

Thanks again to all contributors and have fun!

Oliver
