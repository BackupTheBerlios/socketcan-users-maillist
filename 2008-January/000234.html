You can use candump with a second CAN controller on the same bus to get 
a reliable information whether the CAN frame hits the bus.

Please check trunk/kernel/2.6/Documentation/networking/can.txt chapter 
3.2 for some information about local visibility of CAN frames.

Btw. i'm astonished about your 64 units: Do these units only acknowledge 
CAN frames with CAN-IDs they are listening to? Normally a CAN controller 
ACKs every received CAN frame on the bus.
