The SocketCAN driver for the Softing PCMCIA CAN Hardware (e.g. CANcard2, 
EDICcard2 and friends) are now available in the SocketCAN SVN trunk.
The firmware binaries in rev. 4.6 that are needed to run the hardware 
can be downloaded from the BerliOS SocketCAN file store 
(http://developer.berlios.de/projects/socketcan ).

Thanks to Kurt Van Dijck (EIA Electronics) for the contribution of this 
driver.
Also thanks to Michael Koch from Softing AG who allowed us that the 
binary firmware may be hosted on BerliOS.

Have fun,
Oliver
