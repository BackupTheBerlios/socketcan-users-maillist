AFAICS you are currently only *receiving* CAN frames via your SPI 
interface, right?

If this is your only use-case, it should work.

Btw. your last use-case was to send(!) frames and now you are talking 
about receiving frames.

If you need your SPI *only* for the CAN interface, you should consider 
to implement a fully functional CAN netdevice driver (send & receive) on 
the main processor, and push the CAN frames through the SPI in both 
directions.
