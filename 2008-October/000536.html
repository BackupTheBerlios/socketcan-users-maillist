We have SocketCAN setups in the field since 2004; so we actually
*needed* a way to actually make things fixed, and even in times where
Berlios was a floating, badly defined target. It hat to be deployable,
reproducable, we needed fixed versions and revisions that fitted to
existing customer installations, be it ARM, PowerPC or x86.

When things settle down and become well defined in the Linux *Mainline*,
canutils will follow that and we'll maintain compatiblity to whatever
versions are out there.
