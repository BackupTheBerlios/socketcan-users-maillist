Look into the documentation - or the BIOS setup.

For ISA devices you need to know where they are located in the i/o address
space and which interrupt is connected to the CAN controller chip.


> 
> Do I have to understand and configure "udev" ??

No.

As Wolfgang worte before just provide *the correct module parameters*, when
you invoke

   modprobe sja1000_isa port=... irq=...

When you use 'modprobe' all other needed modules like 'sja1000' and 'can_dev'
are loaded automatically - so you don't need to load whatever you can find in
the CAN modules anyway.
