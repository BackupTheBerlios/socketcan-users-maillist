Hi,
Finally, Thanx to you guys, I am able to recieve data using candump now
the problem in recieve was not from the socketcan side

The irq 3 4 10 and 11 were mapped in the kernel with COM 1 to COM 4 as seen
in this document
http://www.microcomputersystems.com/MSI-6354.pdf

I just disabled from the bios all the COMs from 1 to 4 and the additional
USB ports i.e. 2 and 3 so it disable the board level interrupt association

Thank you very much for you guys to keep in touch and help me out during
this problem.
I am very very thankfull toyou
