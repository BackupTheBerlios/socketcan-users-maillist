> Right. Unfortunately, I do not have such hardware.

I do have several PEAK PCAN-PC/104, an Eurotech com1274 (2x i82527) and a very
old SBS PC7 with onboard i82527, a Gedas Digibox with 4 SJA1000 connected to 2
EPP ports provided by a dual port PCI parport controller (horrible hw design),
a c200 parallelport adapter and a PEAK PCAN-Dongle for the parallel port at work.

At Volkswagen we're currently only using a MPC5200 based hardware for all our
vehicle interfaces an therefore the above interfaces remain unused. The
decreasing number of projects using the PEAK PCAN-PC/104, the Eurotech com1274
and the Digibox are running well with the old driver.

Btw. having drivers that support the standard ioport and iomem access with
plain mapped registers is something that pops up from time to time. From my
perspective supporting the SJA1000 and i82527 with these standard hardware
designs is needed and can be used as a basis for other designs also - if
someone needs it.

I'll take my old SBS PC7 with a 566MHz Celeron, 256MB, onboard i82527 and
PCAN-PC/104 from my shelf and try a dist-upgrade and svn up next week ;-)
