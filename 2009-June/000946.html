Nice to hear that you got it working. Unfortunately, you are using very
old code. As you might have realized, we are currently working on
ISA-bus drivers for the most recent version of Socket-CAN and we could
provide up-to-date drivers for the cards you are using, in case you are
willing to switch. The new version is currently developed under the SVN
branch "branches/netlink" and is in sync with the SJA1000 drivers
showing up with 2.6.31 in the mainline kernel. To get an impression
about the improvements of the new version please read chapter 6.5 of:

http://git.kernel.org/?p=linux/kernel/git/davem/net-next-2.6.git;a=blob_plain;f=Documentation/networking/can.txt;hb=HEAD

Wolfgang.
