Hi all,

as far as i understood Alexandre is asking for a driver that only supports
BasicCAN (with 11 Bit CAN Identifiers!) - like the PCA82C200 - or at least
has the BasicCAN register layout for the SJA1000 that's different to the
SJA1000 PeliCAN register layout we are currently using.

In this case, i would take the sja1000 driver as a starting point and then
create a 82C200 driver from that, that's also capable to run the sja1000 in
BasicCAN compatibility mode.

See upgrade hints from 82c200 to SJA1000

http://www-rocq.inria.fr/syndex/doc/CAN/UPGRADE11.pdf

or

http://www.e-lab.de/downloads/DOCs/PCA82C200.pdf

i just searched from the net.
