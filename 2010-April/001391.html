Historically we distinguish between "basic" and "full" CAN controllers.
In contrast to basic CAN controllers, full CAN controllers allow to do
filtering in hardware via various RX message objects unloading the CPU.
For Socket-CAN, Basic CAN controllers are definitely the better
solution, especially if they provide reasonable buffering of RX
messages, like the SJA1000.
