Hi,

just check out the latest SocketCAN SVN tree:

http://developer.berlios.de/svn/?group_id=6475

in socketcan/trunk/can-utils is all you need.

With 'make' the tools compile and with 'make install' (as root) they are
simply copied to /usr/local/bin

> "Protocol not Supported"
> 
> socket(PF_CAN, SOCK_RAW, CAN_BCM);

As expected :-)

CAN_BCM is not a SOCK_RAW socket type.

See http://lxr.linux.no/#linux+v3.0.1/Documentation/networking/can.txt#L245

Additional to that read-worthy doscumentation you can also get some ideas when
looking at the files in socketcan/trunk/test or socketcan/trunk/can-utils
