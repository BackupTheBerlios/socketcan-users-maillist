there are tree PCI drivers using the SJA1000 in the SocketCAN SVN:

ems_pci, ixxat_pci and peak_pci

I assume all of them to work when you buy them in the appropriate form 
factor.

E.g. the PEAK PCAN-PC/104-Plus is software compatible to the PEAK PCI card:
http://www.peak-system.com/Product-Details.49+M52f92e4fa34.0.html?&L=1&tx_commerce_pi1[catUid]=6&tx_commerce_pi1[showUid]=7

So this could be one of the possibilities for you.
