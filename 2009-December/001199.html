Hi,

some times ago I started to enhance Wireshark to capture and dissect
frames from a SocketCAN. The patches work quite well, but there are
still some open issues to get clear about. Take a look at the list
archives of Libpcap and Wireshark for details. Theres no code yet
submitted to the project trees.

IMHO using Wireshark to inspect raw CAN frames is not a big advantage (I
prefer candump :-)). My intension for the patches was to gain a basis
for implementing CAN protocols like J1939, CanOpen within Wireshark.  

Maybe you guys can give some feedback or like to try?
