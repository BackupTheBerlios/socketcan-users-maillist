Hi Ulrich,

additional to have separate filter capabilities for each open socket the idea
is that you can have multiple (multi-user) CAN applications on your Linux box.

To ensure all the CAN applications have independently the correct view on the
real CAN bus the locally generated massages are looped back into the local
host once they are sent on the wire, see:

http://lxr.linux.no/#linux+v2.6.37/Documentation/networking/can.txt#L176

So with SocketCAN having two or more applications independently using the same
CAN bus is the standard behaviour.
