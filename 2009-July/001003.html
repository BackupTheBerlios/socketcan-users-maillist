Yes, our canopen library does use nanosleep().  We used to get more of these NOHZ messages, but I turned off some CPU-related power management features and I think the amount of NOHZ messages went down.  It could be my imagination though.  I read some posts which seemed to indicate that this message means the CPU is inappropriately sleeping when a soft IRQ is pending, which the NOHZ patch is supposed to prevent.  So that let me to disabling power management stuff as an experiment.

Thanks for the reply, I feel less threatened knowing I am not the only one getting these messages!
