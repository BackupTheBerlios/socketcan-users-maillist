AFAIK dbc Files are used for signal representations (e.g. in automotive 
environments).
It is used by commercial tools like CANalyser and CANoe from Vektor 
Informatik (http://www.vector-worldwide.com) - and they have a dedicated 
CANdb Editor to create and edit .dbc files.
