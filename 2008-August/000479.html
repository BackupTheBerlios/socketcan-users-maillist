> The can microcontroller on the nodes and the pc is a SJA1000, is there

 From my point of view, you have still electrical problems. I assume 
that  the other nodes on the bus do use 1MB/s as well. Some hints and 
questions:

- Does the cable work in an independent setup (with AT91 off)?
- How long is the cable and is ground connected?
- Is the cable terminated properly with 120 Ohm between CAN_L and CAN_H
   at *both* ends?
- Try a lower bitrate.
- Use a CAN bus monitor/analyser
