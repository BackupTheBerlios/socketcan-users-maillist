Alexey Serov wrote:
> Maybe I should calculate and set BTR0 and BTR1 parameters manually? I
> didn't try this, but ECAN527 driver I tried before didn't require such
> parameters anywhere. Can I find somewhere a working example of
> i82527-iomem module parameters?
>   

I personally had some problems with the DMC bit, that usually is derived 
from the clock. For that reason, i added 'force_dmc=1' as module 
parameter. You should probably try it.

Also the bus configuration register may be set different in your setup - 
there is a module parameter for that.

Additionally calculating BTR0 and BTR1 manually may be also a good choice.

With the 'old' i82527 driver you can look in the procfs

/proc/driver/i82527_regs to look how the registers are set (and compare 
it to your working setup).
