Hi Jorge,

unfortunately i'm not familiar with the at91_can driver but i can tell 
you that the statistic above only tells you something about how the 
packets flow through the CAN networklayer.

If you need details (e.g. dropped frames, sent frames) from your 
specific *driver* you should look into

cat /proc/net/dev

or type

/sbin/ifconfig can0

to get the latest state/statistic of your network interface.
