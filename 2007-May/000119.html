Hi Simon,

indeed the socketcan stuff provided in the OSELAS toolchain is derived 
from a very old SocketCAN version.
There is no local loopback functionality and e.g. the basic struct 
can_frame differs a lot.
We had many discussions on the socket-API, on filters of RAW-sockets and 
on these structs which lead to the current SocketCAN which is here on 
BerliOS.
We also have a MPC5200 board here with the OSELAS toolchain and the 
first thing for me was an upgrade from the outdated OSELAS version to 
the current SocketCAN trunk. In the next step, i wanted to use the 
MPC5200 MSCAN driver but in the first step i kicked the ptxdist MSCAN 
driver to work with the current SocketCAN source. Attached you will find 
a diff of this (hacked) OSELAS CAN Driver, which may give you a hint to 
the differences between the two driver smells. As i would like to use 
the BerliOS driver, this driver does not go onto the SVN for a good reason.

Feel free to create a 'real' SocketCAN driver on the basis of the OSELAS 
driver regarding the basic differences addressed in this patch (struct 
can_frame, etc.).

Best regards,
