The only 'real' thing that changed in the sja1000 driver is the support
for the loopback on driver level (see Documentation/networking/can.txt).
This feature can be disabled by providing the module command line
parameter "loopback=0". Maybe you should try this.

But reading your mail this morning, i put my old Celeron 500 brick on
fire( http://www.csi.net.pl/pdf/SBS/PCCOMPACT/pc7.pdf ), took the
current trunk and checked the sja1000 and i82527 (both attached on ISA
bus). Even the receiption and also the sending (cangen -g1 canX) did not
lead to any netdev watchdogs on neither can0, can1 or can2
(sja1000/sja1000/i82527).
