All kernels > 2.6.27 use ARCH=powerpc so CONFIG_PPC_MERGE was removed. To be
able to compile the driver for both older and newer kernel, we define
CONFIG_PPC_MERGE internally for newer kernels.

Signed-off-by: Michal Sojka <sojkam1 at fel.cvut.cz>
