Well, that's the point. According to the errata and some information we found deep in the internet, there is a hardware bug in the FIFO of the ATA-controller. The ATA-FIFO has to be polled to avoid data loss and this is the reason for the high latencies. (source: Freescale MPC5200B Errata)
