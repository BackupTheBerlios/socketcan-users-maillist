On 10/11/2011 11:48 PM, Francois Bouchard wrote:
> Hello me again,
> 
> I was wondering how does the coldfire's Flexcan (for instance for the
> M54418TWR) is designed.
> How many of the 16 messages buffers are dedicated for the reception, how
> many for the broadcast Rx, and finally for the Tx.
> regards,

Freescale has written the driver, ask them.

The mainline flexcan driver uses the RX fifo feature of the new flexcan
cores, which is AFAIC not supported on the coldfire, and I think the
FIFO has a depth of 8 buffers. The mainline driver uses just currently
just one buffer for TX.
